FINAL PROJECT SECTION

For the final project

My web pages detail some key aspects of my life. I have always been a huge Taylor Swift fan, and my 'list page' details which song I think is the most underrated, or deserving of more attention, from each of Swift's albums. My 'restaurants page' lists some of my favorite restaurants from my hometown, and my gallery page depicts one of the most exciting days of my time at Mizzou: our epic win against K-State when we stormed the field. I chose to have a calming theme for my website pages, utilizing a neutral color palette, since most people describe my personality as gentle and calming. Everything is designed to be aesthetically pleasing and easy to follow. Rather than telling one cohesive story, I chose to let the audience into a few important areas of my life. I hope that it conveys me in an accurate, if limited, way. Have fun perusing!
